Frequently Asked Questions

What is Superteam Brazil?

Superteam Brazil is the Brazilian chapter of Superteam, a global network of builders, designers, and creators who are building on Solana. We help you find opportunities, get funding, and learn from the best in the ecosystem.

How do I become a member?

To become a member, you typically need to win a bounty, receive a grant, or be employed by a Solana ecosystem project. You can start by joining our Discord and participating in our community events and bounties.

Do I need to be a developer to join?

Not at all! We actievly recruit designers, content creators, marketers, and business development professionals. Building a Web3 product requires diverse skills.
